== Installation ==

1. Unzip the downloaded .zip file.
2. Upload the The Real Estate Voice plugin folder into the `wp-content/plugins/` directory of your WordPress site.
3. Activate `The Real Estate Voice` from Plugins menu

= Getting started =
The Real Estate Voice is easy to use, once installed, you will see a new menu item "The Real Estate Voice". [This step-by-step guide](https://www.therealestatevoice.com.au/the-real-estate-voice-plugin-installation/) will lead you through the installation and configuration to get you started straight away.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is The Real Estate Voice free? =
The Real Estate Voice is a freemium plugin with a limited library of content and social sharing included with the basic version and a premium subscription that offers automatic posting and scheduling features. [See whats included in the premium version here](https://www.therealestatevoice.com.au/license-plugin/)

= What social media accounts does it work with? =
The plugin seamlessly integrates with your Facebook and Instagram pages. Simply set up your brand details, website URL, and social media accounts in the dashboard, and you’re ready to start sharing content.

= Can I edit the articles? =
Yes, you can edit any of the articles to suit your brand before adding them to your website or sharing on your socials. What’s more, there’s no limit to how many articles you choose to post. Best of all, you can also order custom articles, market reports, suburb profiles, and agent profiles directly from the dashboard.

= What’s the difference between the free and paid version? = 
The free version has a limited library of articles you can add to your website. It doesn’t include the auto post function or auto sharing, plus it doesn’t support sharing listings. The good news is, once you’ve set up and trialled the free version, you’re only one click away from upgrading to the full-featured plugin.
